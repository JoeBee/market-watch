About Market Watch

  • What this is: A ranked list of US stocks scored for a 6–12 month holding window—not a price target or buy/sell signal.
  • How ranking works: Each stock gets a Score from momentum (12‑month return skipping the last month, plus 6‑month return), value (earnings & book yield), and quality (ROE, margins, lower debt).
  • How to read the table: Rank 1 = strongest combined factor profile. Click any row for full metrics, sector leaders, and company summary.
  • Data: Prices from Yahoo/Stooq; fundamentals from Yahoo and SEC EDGAR. Use Fetch Data or Refresh to download updates and re-rank stocks.
  • Tabs: Stock Detail shows all row metrics; Sector Leaders re-ranks vs sector peers; Company Detail adds a factor-based narrative.
  • Column help: Click the ⓘ icon in a column header or detail field for a short description.
